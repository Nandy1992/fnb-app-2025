PANDA

The giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca),also known as the panda bear or simply panda, is a bear species endemic to china.

It is patches around the eyes, ears , legs and shoulder.

Its body is rotund; adult individuals weigh 100 to 115 kg(220 to 254 lb) and are typically 1.2 to 1.9 m (3 ft 11 in 6ft 3 in) long.it is sexually dimorphic, with males being typically 10 to 20% larger than females.
